aam aadmee beema yojana do aktubar do hazar sath ko shuru kee gayi thee yah ek saamaajik suraksha yojana hai jo bhaarat ke nimn-aayei  vaale parivaaro kee orr lakshit hai. iss yojana se un logo ko laabh hota hai jo aam taur par perol par nahin hoatae hain, udaaharan ke lie, machhuaare, auto chaalak, mochee, aadi kam aayei ke kaaran, ve mirtiyu ya vikalaangata jaisee gambheer aakasmikata ka saamna karnei ke liyei paise bachaane ke baare mein nahin sochh sakte hain aam aadmee beema yojana unhen aisee durbhaagyapoorn paristhitiyon ka saamna karane ke lie maudrik sahaayata pradaan karati hai.
