 pradhaanamantree krshi sichaee yojana  har khet ko panee ke aadarsh vaaky ke saath 1 july, 2015 ko lonch kiya gaya. bhaarat sarakaar jal sanrakshan aur isske prabandhan ke liye uchch praathamikata dene ke liye pratibaddh hai. iss prabhaav ke liye pradhaanamantree krshi sichaee yojana (peeemakeesavaee) ko har khet ko paanee kee sinchaee ke vistaar aur paanee ke upayog kee dakshata mein sudhaar ke drshti se taayaar kiya gaya hai. srot nirmaan, vitaran, prabandhan, kshetr anuprayog aur vistaar gatividhiyaan. peeemakeesavaee na keval sunishchit sinchaee ke liye srot banaane par dhyaan kendrit karata hai, balki jal-asanchay aur jal sinachan ke maadhyam se sookshm star par varsha jal ka dohan karake surakshaatmak sinchaee bhee karata hai. prati boond-adhik phasal sunishchit karane ke lie sabsedee ke maadhyam se sookshm sinchaee ko protsaahit kiya jaata hai.
